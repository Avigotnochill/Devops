About Karauli & Dholpur Sandstone

Karauli and Dholpur red sandstone is one of the finest natural stones from Rajasthan, known for its durability, rich red tone, and royal architectural heritage. Used in palaces, forts, temples, and luxury projects across India and abroad.

The Legacy of Karauli & Dholpur Red Sandstone

Karauli and Dholpur red sandstone is a world-renowned natural stone, extracted from the ancient quarries of Rajasthan. Known for its deep red tone, fine grain texture, and exceptional durability, this stone has been the backbone of India’s most iconic architectural masterpieces.

From royal forts and palaces to modern luxury villas and international export projects, Karauli sandstone represents strength, heritage, and timeless elegance. Its ability to withstand extreme weather conditions makes it ideal for both domestic and global construction needs.

Stone Varieties & Grades

Karauli & Dholpur red sandstone is available in multiple grades, classified based on color consistency, surface finish, and structural integrity.

Grade A

Premium Export Quality
  • Uniform deep red color
  • Minimal natural marks
  • Fine grain & high density
  • Precision cutting & finishing
  • Ideal for international export projects

Grade B

Standard Construction Quality
  • Slight color variation
  • Minor natural texture lines
  • Strong and durable stone
  • Used in villas, flooring & facades
  • Cost-effective for large projects

Grade C

Economy & Landscaping
  • Natural color variation
  • Visible texture & grain
  • Good structural strength
  • Used in pathways & boundary walls
  • Best for bulk & budget projects
